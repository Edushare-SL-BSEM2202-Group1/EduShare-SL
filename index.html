About EduShare Sierra Leone

EduShare Sierra Leone is a free, open-source Digital Public Good designed to democratise access to quality educational materials across Sierra Leone. The platform is built by students in BSEM2202 GROUP 1 semester 6 at Limkokwing University of Creative Technology.

Sierra Leone faces a severe shortage of textbooks, lesson materials, and qualified teachers — especially in rural districts. EduShare bridges this gap by creating a central repository where educators and learners can freely share and access resources, with no registration required to download.

🛡️ Data Transparency & Privacy Note

🤝

Voluntary Compliance Framework

EduShare Sierra Leone functions as a non-commercial Digital Public Good. In accordance with standard international data protection best practices:

  • Frictionless Access: No authorization or creation of a user profile record is required to query or download any asset on this network.
  • Data Minimization: Contributing files only tracks the voluntary descriptive string tags typed in the fields above.
  • Legal Foundation: System designs align directly with Sierra Leone's Right to Access Information Act (2013) framework.
